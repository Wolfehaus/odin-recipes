Strawberry Milk

Description

This is an easy homemade strawberry milk recipe for one or two, made with just four ingredients! It is fruity, creamy, subtly sweet, and perfect for any occassion.

This recipe is easy to make and low cost! Enjoy often!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 1/2 cup hulled and chopped fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup of granulated white sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Directions

  1. Step 1:Pour the 1 cup of whole milk and 1 cup of half and half into freezer in order to thoroughly chill
  2. Step 2:In a small pot, combine strawberries, sugar, and water. Bring to a low boil and cook, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes, until fragrant and reduced.
  3. Step 3:Add the strawberry syrup to your combined milk and cream in a glass milk bottle. Begin with 2/3 of the syrup and then add more to taste.
  4. Step 4:Keep chilled in fridge or over ice until ready to serve. Enjoy!
