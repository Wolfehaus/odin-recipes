Bread Pudding

Description

Bread pudding is a popular bread-based dessert made all over the world. It can be savoury or sweet. Its base is generally composed of a few key ingredients: stale bread, milk or cream, eggs, and some form of fat, like butter. It is easy to make and delicious!

This recipe uses milk and unsalted butter. It will take roughly 1 1/2 hours to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 loaf of stale bread (challah or brioche preferred)
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Steps

  1. Step 1:Pre-heat your oven to 350 degrees
  2. Step 2:In a small saucepan over low heat, warm milk, butter, vanilla, sugar, and salt. continue cooking just until butter melts; cool. Meanwhile, butter a 4-to-6 cup baking dish and fill it with cubed bread.
  3. Step 3:Add eggs to cooled milk mixture and whisk; pour mixture over bread. Let the bread-milk mixture sit at room temperature for 1 hour.
  4. Step 4:Bake the bread-milk custard for 30 to 45 minutes, or until custard sets but is still a little wobbly. The edges of the bread should be browned.
  5. Step 5:Serve warm or at room temperature and enjoy!
